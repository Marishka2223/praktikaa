Horikawa is reliable!

Horikawa store is one of the leading suppliers of musical instruments, offering a wide range of products for professionals and amateurs. Located in the city center, Horikawa attracts musicians from all over the region with its extensive selection of instruments and high level of service.





Horikawa presents instruments for all musical styles and tastes. From classical pianos and violins to modern electric guitars and synthesizers, the store meets the needs of even the most demanding customers.
